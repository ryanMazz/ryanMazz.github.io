Problem Statement: Busy Schedule with Confusion

Students are busy and managing time is important. This is not helped by the fact that different professors may use completely different systems for managing classwork and homework. It is confusing when homework for one class is submitted by email while another is submitted on Blackboard and another on dropbox.

Affinity Diagram

In this diagram, we mapped the major parts of our solution to the problem listed above. Thhe basic idea is a web app that consolidates data from other apps and handle interactions between them. The biggest issue we found was with implementation, the necessary data hooks require work on both ends. The biggest benefit is simplicity and ease of use, which is the main focus of our solution.
