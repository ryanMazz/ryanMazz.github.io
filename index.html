Personal Values

Ryan Mazzella

Loyalty

Loyalty is the idea that your word holds value, or that you will keep your promises.

Integrity

Integrity is the idea that you will do what is right, rather than what will benefit you personally.

Intelligence

To me, intelligence is the idea of seeking out knowledge as well as aknowledging that there is always more to learn

Patience

Patience is the idea that you stay steadfast in your actions and do not easily give up. You must be willing to wait if you must.
